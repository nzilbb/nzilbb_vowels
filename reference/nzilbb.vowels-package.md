# nzilbb.vowels: Vowel Covariation Tools

Tools to support research on vowel covariation. Methods are provided to
support Principal Component Analysis workflows (as in Brand et al.
(2021)
[doi:10.1016/j.wocn.2021.101096](https://doi.org/10.1016/j.wocn.2021.101096)
and Wilson Black et al. (2023)
[doi:10.1515/lingvan-2022-0086](https://doi.org/10.1515/lingvan-2022-0086)
).
